Air Jordan 17 Retro

Released on June 2002 in white and university blue. These shoes were designed by Wilson Smith the 3rd. My favorite Air Jordan release of all time, they have never been re-released! I don't know what Nike's problem is but these shoes are very nice, for a low-cut shoe they were ahead of their time and were very casual for a basketball shoe. They held a certain look that just could not be contended with. Easily interchangeable with a basketball jersey or a dress shirt, I would give anything to own a pair in 2019. I honestly think a petition needs to be written up or something because there is a reason they have released every other colorway of the same shoe but not these. Like the sales reflect the success of these shoes and they don't want to have a retail sale wipe out and have to restock them two or three times. The design was way ahead of its time and very futuristic for a shoe released so long ago, making an artificial sock for the shoe was an awesome idea and adds to how cool they look. NIKE PLEASE RE-RELASE THIS SHOE! The video is a collector who found a deadstock pair that he purchased and unboxed in 2014.
